Pocket Mirror

A Free RPG Mystery Horror Game

Review Score:

The tale begins when a young girl wakes up and begins exploring a mysterious world. She has lost her memory and is trying to figure out where she is and who she is. Her only keepsake that clues in on her identity is a pocket mirror. Help her uncover her true identity!
