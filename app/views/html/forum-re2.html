Resident Evil 2

About the Game:

Review Score: 5/5

The second entry in the Resident Evil series. It's Leon first day as a a police officer in Raccoon City and when he arrives at the city he finds Claire who arrived in the city at the same time looking for her brother Chris Redfield. The two of them have to survive the night facing against zombies and horrible monstrosities. What could have caused this outbreak?
