Dead Space 2

A Monster of a Sequel

Review Score:

The second entry in the Dead Space series. Three years after Dead Space, Isaac Clarke awakens in a space station called the "Sprawl." There is a new necropmorph oubreak aboard the Sprawl and it is up to Isaac to stop it and uncover the truth of the outbreak and the dimentia he is now experiencing.


Enter the Sprawl

The threat facing Isaac is no longer confined to a singular space station. You'll face Necromorphs in Zero G environments. Strap on your suit boosters, blast through the Church of Unitology, and jet across the Solar Array.


Fight a deadlier enemy

New Necromorph breeds await, including the stealthy Stalker strain and creepy, infested children known as The Pack. Use your unconventional arsenal of traditional firearms, like the mine-shooting Detonator, and weaponized engineering tools, like the rapid-fire Rivet Gun, and you just might make it out alive.


Are you crazy?

Delve into the mysteries surrounding the Necromorph infestation and learn more about Isaac Clarke's tortured past. You may even catch a glimpse of the elusive engineer's face as he interacts with the precious few survivors aboard The Sprawl.
