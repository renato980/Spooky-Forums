Silent Hill 2

About the Game:

Review Score: 5/5

The next entry in the Silent Hill series. One day James sunderland recieves a letter from his wife asking him to visit her in their special place in Silent hill. But, Mary has been dead for over a year. Is Mary really there waiting for him? Fight through the horrifying monsters of silent hill to uncover the truth.


Reviews

The best horror game of all time period. Amazing story with a twist you never saw coming, horrifying monsters, amazing music/sound design, great voice actors. New horror games don't compare to this amazing game.
