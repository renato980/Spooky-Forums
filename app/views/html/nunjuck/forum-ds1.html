Dead Space

About the Game:

Review Score: 5/5

The USG Ishimura sends out a distress signal and the Kellion is sent to investigate. When their ship malfunctions and crashes into the Ishimura they encounter horrific monstrtosities. Take control of engineer Isaac Clarke and survive against these grotesque monsters and escape the Ishimura.

Reviews

Review 1

This is such an amazing game, the graphics are great, the combat is amazing, and it is extremely scary as well. You never know when one of the monsters will appear as you explore the ship.

Review 2

Rebuilt from the ground up, Capcom's survival horror classic returns better and bloodier than ever. Featuring a stunning visual presentation, updated controls and immersive audio design, Resident Evil 2 brings the zombie-infested Raccoon City Police Department to life (and death) like never before. Offering a bone-chilling blend of cryptic puzzles, resource conservation and encounters with infected freaks, this one is best enjoyed with the lights down and the volume up.
