Ib

About The Game:

Review Score: 5.0

A game made in RPG maker. Take control of Ib who goes on a trip to an art museum with her parents. There is a sudden black out and everyone is gone. She is led by paint splatters on the ground to a painting that takes her to a world within the paintings. Help her escape this world and reunite with her parents.
