Alien: Isolation

About the Game:

Review Score: 5.0

15 years after the original Alien film, Amanda Ripley learns of a flight recorder from her mother's ship is being held in a space station called "Sevastopol". Once onboard she disovers that the space station is out of control due to an alien creature. Help her uncover the truth of alien's arrival at the space station and escape.
