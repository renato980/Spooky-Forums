Silent Hill 2

Game Overview

Review Score:

The second entry in the Silent Hill series. One day, James Sunderland recieves a letter from his wife asking him to visit her in their special place in Silent Hill. Mary has been dead for over a year though! Is Mary really there waiting for him? Fight through the horrifying monsters of Silent Hill to uncover the truth.
